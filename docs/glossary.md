# CMF Glossary

A concise reference for key terms used throughout the Coherence Mapping Framework (CMF).

---

### **Coherence**
A stable relational pattern between elements of a system.

### **Mapping**
A directed relationship connecting one element or field to another.

### **Field**
A domain of meaning or influence within a CMF object.

### **Node**
A discrete unit that can hold structure, metadata, or relationships.

### **Operator**
A transformation applied to a CMF object or its components.

### **Coherence Object**
A structured representation containing nodes, fields, mappings, and metadata.

### **Pattern**
A recurring relational structure that appears across CMF objects.

### **Metadata**
Humanâ€‘readable descriptive information about an object or operator.

### **Structure**
The internal organization of a CMF object.

### **Versioning**
Tracking changes to CMF objects or operators over time.

### **Trace**
An analytical operator that follows mappings to reveal dependencies.

### **Normalize**
A composite operator that brings an object into canonical form.

### **Resolve**
A composite operator that eliminates contradictions or incoherence.
